## TOOL-SPECIFIC BEST PRACTICES

### ChatGPT Best Practices:
- Use specific, detailed prompts with clear constraints
- Always request multiple variations for A/B testing
- Include character limits and formatting requirements
- Specify target audience and intent clearly

### Jasper.ai Best Practices:
- Start with appropriate document templates
- Use tone and style settings consistently
- Apply multiple optimization passes
- Save successful variations as custom templates

### Frase.io Best Practices:
- Regular competitor audits (weekly minimum)
- Focus on 3-5 primary competitors per keyword
- Export content briefs for implementation teams
- Track content gap closure over time

### SurferSEO Best Practices:
- Audit content quarterly for major keywords
- Monitor algorithm updates and adjust accordingly
- Use content editor for real-time optimization
- Benchmark against 5-10 top-ranking pages

This integrated workflow ensures systematic, data-driven SEO optimization using the full power of AI tools while maintaining quality and compliance standards.
